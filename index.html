Cyber security is what you use to protect all of your data across the internet, including your devices, sensitive information, and files.

Cyber security is the practice of online protection. Without it, everyone's information would be less secure, exposing them to risks such as ransomware and online attacks.

Cyber security protects your systems and networks, ensuring data integrity and confidentiality while allowing safe operation on digital platforms.

Passwords are a common way to protect accounts. Strong passwords should include lowercase, uppercase, symbols, and numbers. In fact, a strong password once saved me from school when I was nearly being hacked.

Using software like VPNs hides your location, runs virus scans, updates drivers, and blocks malware from malicious websites.

Cyber security awareness helps everyone feel safe online without worrying about hacking, malware breaches, or personal information leaks.
